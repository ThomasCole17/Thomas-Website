My Website About Portsmouth

I am from a city in England called Portsmouth. It is a really nice place to stay because I think the weather is really nice. You can also ride a daily ferry to the Isle Of Wight. It's a really short journey that takes about 40 mins. Youy can take really nice walks along the seaside. There is also a place where you can get a lovely hot chocolate in a place called the Tenth Hole Tea Rooms.

The main reason why it is called the Tenth Hole is because it is built next to a golf course that has 9 holes. So after you play golf, you will visit the last hole (the tenth one).
