Portsmouth

There is also a place where you can get a lovely hot chocolate in a place called the Tenth Hole Tea Rooms.

The main reason why it is called the Tenth Hole is because it is built next to a golf course that has 9 holes. So after you play golf, you will visit the final, tenth hole. There is a wide selection of delicious cakes and desserts each one being equally fantastic. https://www.thetenthhole.co.uk/ Here is a link to the website of this cafe!



The local football team is called Portsmouth FC. They are celebrating their 125th aniversary and are doing really well in their league. They hold the record for the longest holders of the FA cup.
